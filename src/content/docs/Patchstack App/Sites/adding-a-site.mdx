Adding a site to Patchstack is relatively easy. Here is a simple guide on how to add and connect a site to Patchstack App:
<Steps>
1. Navigate to the <a href="https://app.patchstack.com/sites" target="_blank">**Sites**</a> view in Patchstack App
2. Click on **+ Add new** button. ![](@images/patchstack-sites-add-new.png)
3. Type your domain name into the popup ![](@images/patchstack-add-first-site-dialogue.png)
4. Click on **Continue to plugin sync**.  
5. You will be taken to the next step, where you can follow the next instructions. ![](@images/patchstack-checking-sync-status.png)
6. Click **Download latest plugin**
7. Upload the plugin to your WordPress site by visiting /wp-admin > Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin
8. Activate the plugin
</Steps>
If you need further help, don't hesitate to reach out to us via live chat at the bottom right corner!
